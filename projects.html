Description

In this project, I worked in a team of 8 students to create an Android app in Xamarin, as well as a back-end staff portal using ASP .Net Core MVC with Razor Pages. The app was made for the non-profit organization Friends of the Saskatoon Aforestation Areas, Inc. We developed an app to create and show virtual "park signs" in the two parks that the group owned.
