Description

In this project, I worked in a team of 8 students to create an Android app in Xamarin, as well as a back-end staff portal using ASP .Net Core MVC with Razor Pages. The app was made for the non-profit organization Friends of the Saskatoon Aforestation Areas, Inc. We developed an app to create and show virtual "park signs" in the two parks that the group owned.

I learned how to apply test-driven development (we used Selenium, Appium, Nunit, and Xunit for testing) and agile principles through participating in standup meetings and working in Sprints with stories and scenarios. I also learned how to work in a team and use Git effectively. This projet really helped me appreciate and learn to deal with scope creep and managing time and expectations. I feel like I am now more able to estimate such constraints more easily aftere working on this project.

Screenshots were taken from the production environment, on an emulator. You can download the app to see some of these screens in action, though the web pages are private, so only these screen shots are available.
